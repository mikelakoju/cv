Dr. Mike Lakoju

Data Science and Cyber Analytics Researcher

In my current role, I function as a Research Associate in Data Science and Cyber Analytics in the School of Computer Science & Informatics -Cardiff University-. I am part of the Security, Privacy and Human Factors Research group. My current research -"Chatty Factories"- is a £1.5m project focused on revolutionizing the UK manufacturing industry by creating a system which allows products securely "talk" directly to the factory floor thereby allowing the possibility of harnessing product use data in real time. My focus in the research involves Cyber Security; Information Technology Security & Security Architecture Modelling/ Developing; Understanding the risk, threats, vulnerabilities of connecting IT with Industry control systems (ICS/ SCADA); Operational Technology (ICS/SCADA) Security; using Machine Learning to identify patterns; Exploring IoT with Python and MATLAB; Risk Assessment; Internet of Things; Big Data Visualization using tools Like Tableau and Python seaborn, Matplotlib & Plotly; Features reduction (PCA); Presentation to Industry stakeholders; Leading/ Creating Data flows model for the Chatty concept; GDPR research considerations for the Chatty concept; Natural Language Processing (NLP), Evaluating the performance of each of these Classification models: Naive Bayes, Decision Tree and Random Forest, etc.
